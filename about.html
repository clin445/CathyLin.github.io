Recently, I have been working more on UI for games, which inclues making title art for the home page of games and creating mockups and wireframes for websites. There is still a lot that I want to learn when creating different websites or games that will be seen by others, but I believe that I also can share my unique outlooks and personal styles when it comes to front end or graphic design.

My website is till under construction as I am always working on my portfolio, and I cannot wait to show you more! I am very pleased to meet you, and I hope that we can get in contact soon!
